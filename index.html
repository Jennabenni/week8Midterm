Activity Planner

This website acts as a multi-page activity planner for when someone wants an activity to do. This project allows users to select their preferences with a quiz and suggest an activity based on the result, schedule when the activity is taking place, make notes about the activity or future/past activities, and creates a checklist based on the activity chosen of items the user may need.
